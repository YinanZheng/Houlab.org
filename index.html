Dr. Hou's Population Epigenetics Laboratory is located in the Department of Preventive Medicine at Northwestern University Feinberg School of Medicine. Since 2012 the laboratory has expanded into a research team incorporating experts in epidemiology, cancer biology, genetics, epigenetics, bioinformatics, and biostatistics as well as a network of research collaborators spanning the globe and including data from population cohorts that includes Americans, Mexicans, Chinese, Lebanese, Tanzanians, Nigerians, Amish, Veterans, and neonates among other countries and population groups.
